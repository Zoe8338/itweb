Our Services

Network Setup

From UniFi mesh Wi-Fi to TP-Link routers, we install and configure fast, secure, and reliable networks for your space. Ideal for both homes and small offices.

PBX Telephony

We install and configure Asterisk- or FreePBX-based phone systems, allowing internal extensions, call forwarding, IVRs, voicemail-to-email, and more.

Starlink Integration

We professionally install Starlink equipment and optimize placement, including custom pole mounts or wall brackets when necessary. Perfect for rural areas.

Servers & NAS

Deploy Proxmox, TrueNAS, or Synology setups to enable backups, shared folders, and local cloud storage. Great for collaborative teams and home labs.

Ticketing Systems

Deploy open-source tools like osTicket or Zammad to manage IT support workflows, prioritize client issues, and keep teams organized.

Live Chat & Websites

We integrate live chat tools like LiveKit or Rocket.Chat into your website and offer simple, effective site hosting using GitHub Pages, WordPress, or static HTML.
